Quinoa veg salad

A high protein vegetarian salad. It can be served warm or cold.

Ingredients:
  • Quinoa
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Walnuts
  • Sunflower seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook 1 cup quinoa with 2 cups of water
  2. Toss quinoa with all the vegetables and feta in a bowl
  3. Drizzle some olive oil, 1 tsp of lemon juice, salt and pepper
  4. Complete the salad dressing with walnuts and sunflower seeds
High protein quinoa salad is ready to enjoy!!!
